# ShadowStrike Suite

**Professional digital forensics tools built by a practitioner, for practitioners.**

ShadowStrike is a family of open-source tools spanning two product lines — one for serious casework, one for competition. Every tool in the suite was born from a real gap identified in the field.

---

## Product Lines

### Strategos Suite
*Professional digital forensic investigations.*

Named for *Strategos Autokrator* — the title of supreme command held by Philip II and Alexander the Great.

| Product | Package | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Nika** | [`niki`](https://pypi.org/project/niki) | Forensic parser — full spectrum evidence ingestion |
| **Komanda** | [`komanda`](https://pypi.org/project/komanda) | Case management |
| **Niska** | [`niska`](https://pypi.org/project/niska) | Collaborative investigation review |
| **Sila** | [`sila`](https://pypi.org/project/sila) | Evidence management with immutable chain of custody |
| **Vistina** | [`vistina`](https://pypi.org/project/vistina) | Legal review and forensic-to-legal production |

### Megdan CTF Line
*Digital forensics CTF competition toolkit.*

Named for *Megdan* — the historic Macedonian field of contest where duels were decided.

| Product | Package | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Sarissa** | [`sarissa`](https://pypi.org/project/sarissa) | Rapid-access CTF dashboard |
| **Treska** | [`treska`](https://pypi.org/project/treska) | Fast parser for zipped logical extractions |
| **Poligon** | [`poligon`](https://pypi.org/project/poligon) | Practice simulator and training environment |

---

## Why ShadowStrike exists

Every major forensic tool on the market — iLEAPP, aLEAPP, mac_apt, APOLLO, AXIOM, Arsenic — was tested against zipped logical extractions, the dominant evidence format in both CTF competitions and real-world mobile investigations. All six failed to handle them efficiently.

ShadowStrike was built to fill that gap.
